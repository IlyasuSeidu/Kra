# KPIs

## Reliability KPIs
- Delivery completion rate.
- Lost package rate.
- Failed handoff rate.

## Speed KPIs
- Dispatch turnaround time.
- Average inter-station transit time.
- Final-mile completion time.

## Trust KPIs
- Dispute rate.
- Refund rate.
- Proof capture completion rate.

## Growth KPIs
- Repeat sender rate.
- Active station count.
- Delivery volume per route.

## Approved KPI Targets
- delivery completion rate: `>= 97%`
- lost package rate: `<= 0.5%`
- failed handoff rate: `<= 1%`
- dispatch turnaround compliance: `>= 90%`
- dispute rate: `<= 3%`
- proof capture completion rate: `>= 98%`

## Measurement Windows
- daily for station health
- weekly for route and reliability
- monthly for growth and repeat sender review

## Ownership
- reliability and speed: `ops_admin`
- payment and refund KPIs: `finance_admin`
- growth metrics: `business owner`

## Change Rule
- KPI targets may change only after `30 days` of pilot data and a logged decision update.
